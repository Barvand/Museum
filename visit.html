How do i get there?

By foot

It takes 15-20 min to walk from the city sentrum to the science museum. It is a nice walk through the park, Nygårdsparken, but there are many different routs to choose from. Take a look at the map at the bottom.

Bergen City Bike

The City Bike i s an easy way to get around the city, and a good alternative to bus/lightrail. You need to download and app that will tell you where you will find available bikes.

Bus and light rail

The nearest bus and light rail stop is Florida which is within 5 minutes walking distance from the science museum. Lines stopping at Florida:

Bybanen Line 1, Bergen Light Rail

Bus Line 12

Bus Line 13

You can use the Skyss website for route planning and buying tickets

Admission

The entrance is free, but booking is required

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

The museum is accessible for all

Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics?

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drinks

We will not let you starve!

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Enjoy our science shop!

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
