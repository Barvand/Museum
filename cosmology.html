Discover gravity

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by. Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

Embarking on Cosmic Voyages with Virtual Reality Glasses

With VR glasses, the cosmos becomes an interactive playground. Navigate through asteroid belts, witness the birth and death of stars up close, and even travel back in time to witness pivotal cosmic events. These glasses, equipped with advanced simulations and data from telescopes and space probes, allow us to experience the universe in a way that was once the privilege of a select few.

Kinetic energy

Experience kinectic energy first hand, kinetic energy is the energy associated with the motion of an object and is determined by its mass and velocity. It is a fundamental concept in physics and is used in many practical applications to analyze and describe the behavior of moving objects.
